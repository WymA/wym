ORION

A large and bright constellation on the equator, between thestars Aldebaran and Sirius. It contains a remarkable nebula visibleto the naked eye.The flaming glories of Orion's belt. E. Everett.

PROBE

An instrument for examining the depth or other circumstances ofa wound, ulcer, or cavity, or the direction of a sinus, of forexploring for bullets, for stones in the bladder, etc. Parr. Probe,or Probe-pointed, scissors (Surg.), scissors used to open wounds, theblade of which, to be thrust into the orifice, has a button at theend. Wiseman.

TEREBRATULA

A genus of brachiopods which includes many living and somefossil species. The larger valve has a perforated beak, through whichprojects a short peduncle for attachment. Called also lamp shell.
