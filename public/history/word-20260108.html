GINGLYMODI

An order of ganoid fishes, including the modern gar pikes andmany allied fossil forms. They have rhombic, ganoid scales, aheterocercal tail, paired fins without an axis, fulcra on the fins,and a bony skeleton, with the vertebræ convex in front and concavebehind, forming a ball and socket joint. See Ganoidel.
