SAUBA ANT

A South American ant (Ecodoma cephalotes) remarkable for havingtwo large kinds of workers besides the ordinary ones, and for theimmense size of its formicaries. The sauba ant cuts off leaves ofplants and carries them into its subterranean nests, and thus oftendoes great damage by defoliating trees and cultivated plants.
