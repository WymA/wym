GODWIT

One of several species of long-billed, wading birds of thegenus Limosa, and family Tringidæ. The European black-tailed godwit(Limosa limosa), the American marbled godwit (L. fedoa), theHudsonian godwit (L. hæmastica), and others, are valued as gamebirds. Called also godwin.
