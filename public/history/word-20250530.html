INDUSIAL

Of, pertaining to, or containing, the petrified cases of thelarvæ of certain insects. Indusial limestone (Geol.), a fresh-waterlimestone, largely composed of the agglomerated cases of caddiceworms, or larvæ of caddice flies (Phryganea). It is found in Miocenestrata of Auvergne, France, and some other localities.
