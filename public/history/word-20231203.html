INTERCALARY

Inserted or introduced among others in the calendar; as, anintercalary month, day, etc.; -- now applied particularly to the oddday (Feb. 29) inserted in the calendar of leap year. See Bissextile,n.

POLYPODIUM

POLYPODIUM

A genus of plants of the order Filices or ferns. Thefructifications are in uncovered roundish points, called sori,scattered over the inferior surface of the frond or leaf. There arenumerous species.

PYROPHYLLITE
