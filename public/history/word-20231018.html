SYNTHESIS

The art or process of making a compound by putting theingredients together, as contrasted with analysis; thus, water ismade by synthesis from hydrogen and oxygen; hence, specifically, thebuilding up of complex compounds by special reactions, whereby theircomponent radicals are so grouped that the resulting substances areidentical in every respect with the natural articles when such occur;thus, artificial alcohol, urea, indigo blue, alizarin, etc., are madeby synthesis.
