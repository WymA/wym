PRONGHORN

An American antelope (Antilocapra Americana), native of theplain near the Rocky Mountains. The upper parts are mostly yellowishbrown; the under parts, the sides of the head and throat, and thebuttocks, are white. The horny sheath of the horns is shed annually.Called also cabrée, cabut, prongbuck, and pronghorned antelope.
