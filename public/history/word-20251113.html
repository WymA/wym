FLAX

A plant of the genus Linum, esp. the L. usitatissimum, whichhas a single, slender stalk, about a foot and a half high, with blueflowers. The fiber of the bark is used for making thread and cloth,called linen, cambric, lawn, lace, etc. Linseed oil is expressed fromthe seed.
