VEGETABLE

Divided into two subclasses:Angiosperms, having the woody fiber interspersed with dotted orannular ducts, and the seed contained in a true ovary; Gymnosperms,having few or no ducts in the woody fiber, and the seeds naked. 2.Monocotyledons (called also Endogens).-- Seeds with single cotyledon.
