DICTOGRAPH

#30185

CEMENTATION

CEMENTATION

A process which consists in surrounding a solid body with thepowder of other substances, and heating the whole to a degree notsufficient to cause fusion, the physical properties of the body beingchanged by chemical combination with powder; thus iron becomes steelby cementation with charcoal, and green glass becomes porcelain bycementation with sand.
