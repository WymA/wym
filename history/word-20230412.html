VESTLET

Any one of several species of actinians belonging to the genusCerianthus. These animals have a long, smooth body tapering to thebase, and two separate circles of tentacles around the mouth. Theyform a tough, flexible, feltlike tube with a smooth internal lining,in which they dwell, whence the name.
