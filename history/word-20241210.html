MOSS

A cryptogamous plant of a cellular structure, with distinctstem and simple leaves. The fruit is a small capsule usually openingby an apical lid, and so discharging the spores. There are manyspecies, collectively termed Musci, growing on the earth, on rocks,and trunks of trees, etc., and a few in running water.
